# Welcome to the wunderful world of Latin Grammar

This wiki is aimed at providing a good resource for the latin grammar. Each article provides well written explanations, examples and in-depth references 
to word-types, special constructions like the *ablativus absolutus*, word-endings and irregular stems and much much more.

The articles explain:

- Wordtypes
- Sentence analysis
- Grammatical constructs
- Tenses, Cases, Voices, etc.
- Particles
- ...
